Ready Player One Essay

This is a brief excerpt from my essay about Ready Player One. In my CART English 11 class, we were tasked with producing an argumentative essay about why Ready Player One should be used more often in high schools. We were required to format the essay using MLA styling. The reason I selected the sample was that we were given only the time in class to finish it; in addition, it is one of my most recent works of writing making it much more relevant than older ones.
